Department Of CSE |

Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science & Engineering at IIIT Nagpur

IIIT Nagpur offers a B.Tech. program in Computer Science & Engineering, featuring an integrated curriculum. Core subjects include programming languages, computer architecture, operating systems, and theoretical computer science and mathematics.

Advanced topics like artificial intelligence, natural language processing, algorithm analysis, databases, and soft skills are covered in higher semesters. This balanced approach ensures students are well-prepared for both applied and theoretical advancements in the field of computer science.

B.Tech. in CSE (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning) at IIIT Nagpur

The B.Tech. in Computer Science Engineering (AI & ML) at IIIT Nagpur is a highly prestigious degree. This program builds a solid foundation in AI and ML through the study of logic, knowledge representation, probabilistic models, and machine learning.AI aims to simulate human intellect and behavior, while machine learning allows systems to make predictions and decisions based on past data without explicit programming. Students will learn to design and develop knowledge-based systems, artificial life, robotics, and neural networks.

Studies show that approximately 30% of B2B organizations will use AI to enhance at least one sales process, indicating high market demand. Compared to traditional engineering disciplines, a B.Tech. in AI & ML offers substantially higher returns

B.Tech. in CSE (Data Science & Analytics) at IIIT Nagpur

The B.Tech. in Computer Science Engineering (Data Science & Analytics) at IIIT Nagpur focuses on the fundamentals of computer science and data analytics, emphasizing problem-solving and engineering skills through computational approaches.Students explore modeling, analysis, and extraction of actionable insights for real-world challenges, and are encouraged to employ data visualization techniques for informed decision-making. The curriculum covers programming, statistics, mathematical reasoning, machine learning, knowledge discovery, communication, and visualization skills.

The department boasts highly skilled faculty, state-of-the-art laboratories, and relevant learning materials. The program is designed to meet the ever-evolving industry demands, fostering comprehensive learning and active student participation.

B.Tech. in CSE (Human-Computer Interaction & Gaming Technology) at IIIT Nagpur

Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) focuses on the design and usability of computer technology, specifically the interfaces between users and devices. HCI research explores how people interact with technology and aims to make these interactions more intuitive and efficient. This multidisciplinary field involves collaboration with experts in engineering, psychology, linguistics, and social sciences.

The study of computer games has expanded beyond entertainment to include educational, therapeutic, and simulation applications. HCI plays a crucial role in developing better games by assessing user interactions and behaviors during gameplay. This research benefits both individuals and society by advancing game design and applying gaming concepts in innovative ways.

Department Of ECE |

B.Tech. in Electronics & Communication Engineering at IIIT Nagpur

The Department of Electronics and Communication at IIIT Nagpur offers a B.Tech. in Electronics & Communication Engineering. The program fosters well-rounded development by exposing students to a cutting-edge curriculum, including Embedded Systems, Internet of Things, Communication Technologies, Control Systems, Image and Computer Vision, CMOS Design, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence.

To keep pace with rapidly evolving technologies, the curriculum is updated every semester. Rigorous lab sessions and projects reinforce concepts and provide hands-on experience.

B.Tech. in ECI (Internet of Things) at IIIT Nagpur

The B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering (Internet of Things) at IIIT Nagpur focuses on the vast scope of IoT applications. IoT refers to a network of internet-connected devices used in various fields such as urban transport, medical devices, home electronics, and more, enhancing human life.

The program provides a solid theoretical foundation, professional knowledge, and practical skills in IoT. Students gain expertise in electronic engineering, computer science, wireless communication, and computer networks. Additionally, they develop strong skills in sensors & instrumentation, chip design, and IoT privacy and security
