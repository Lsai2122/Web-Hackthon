IIITN DIRECTOR

Dr.Prem Lal Patel

Professor Prem Lal Patel,the Director of VNIT Nagpur, also took on the role of Director at IIIT Nagpur in October 2024. He has a distinguished background in Civil Engineering, specializing in Hydraulics and Water Resources. Before joining SVNIT Surat in 2007, he severd at Delhi College of Engineering and the Border Roads Organization.


With degrees from Government Engineering College, Rewa and IIT Roorke, Professor Patel's research covers river mechanics, flood control, and climate change impacts on Water systems,He has guided numerous theses and projects, published over 260 papers, and is a member of several technical societies. he has also set up a Centre of Excellence on Water Resources & Flood Management at SVNIT with a grant from the World Bank.
