Why hire from IIIT Nagpur ?


Admission and Academics|

IIIT Nagpur stands as a premier institute of national importance, backed by the Government of Maharashtra and industry partners. It admits only the top 2% of JEE Mains candidates through JoSAA, ensuring a pool of highly talented students. The curriculum is meticulously designed by industry experts, incorporating core subjects from the first semester and offering a Choice-Based Credit System, which allows students to tailor their academic journey. With mandatory industry internships, credit-based online certification opportunities, and frequent workshops on cutting-edge technologies like Machine Learning, Data Structures, and Robotics, students graduate with strong technical expertise and practical experience.

Industry Collaboration|

Industry-academia collaboration is a cornerstone of IIIT Nagpur, with partnerships like Tata Consultancy Services Ltd. and MoUs with ESIEE Paris for exchange programs. Research at IIITN is driven by industry needs, focusing on areas such as Deep Learning, IoT, Wireless Communication, Biomedical Signal Processing, and Nanotechnology. These research-driven initiatives ensure that graduates possess innovative problem-solving skills and hands-on experience with emerging technologies.

Research and Innovation|

Beyond academics, IIIT Nagpur nurtures a well-rounded student community through vibrant technical and cultural clubs. The DotSlash programming community, Machine Learning Group, and Competitive Programming Group provide students with platforms to enhance their coding skills, while the #Ack-Z ethical hacking club and gaming communities foster technical creativity. On the cultural front, clubs such as the Music Club, Talartsy (Art Club), and Beats Spark (Dance Club) promote creativity and self-expression. Additionally, the REAP Research Club and 7C Public Speaking Club help students develop research acumen, communication, and leadership skills.

Events and Industry Exposure

The institute also hosts two major annual events: TantraFiesta, the flagship technical fest, and Abhivyakti, the grand cultural fest, which serve as platforms for innovation, networking, and showcasing talent.


By recruiting from IIIT Nagpur, companies gain access to exceptionally skilled, research-driven, and industry-ready professionals equipped with both technical expertise and holistic development.
