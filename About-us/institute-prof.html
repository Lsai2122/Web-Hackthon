Institute Profile

Introduction

The Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Nagpur is one of the IIIT under the Indian Institute of Information Technology (Public-Private Partnership) Act, 2017. IIIT Nagpur is a distinguished "Institution of National Importance." Initially operating from a temporary campus, the Institute shifted to its permanent location in Butibori, Nagpur.

IIITN fosters innovation and entrepreneurship, providing high-quality education and engaging in industry-relevant research in Information Technology and allied fields. The Institute emphasizes public-private partnerships to drive cutting-edge research and development.

Its mission is to nurture youth, meeting the nation's knowledge needs, and offering global leadership in technology and innovation. IIITN is committed to becoming a premier institution and a leading research center, with a strong focus on social and environmental orientation.
